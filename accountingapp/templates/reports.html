Financial Reports

Sales Report

The sales report is an essential tool for tracking the revenue generated from goods or services over a specified period. It typically includes metrics such as total sales volume, average transaction value, and sales growth compared to previous periods. By analyzing sales trends, businesses can identify their best-performing products and services, as well as seasonal fluctuations in demand.

Profit & Loss Report

The profit and loss report, often referred to as the income statement, provides a holistic view of a company's financial performance by detailing revenues, costs, and expenses incurred during a specific period. The report calculates net profit or loss by subtracting total expenses from total revenues. This information is crucial for stakeholders to assess the company's ability to generate profit and manage its expenses efficiently.
